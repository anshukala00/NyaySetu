# Nyaysetu - Citizen User Guide

Welcome to Nyaysetu, your digital gateway to justice. This guide will help you navigate the citizen portal and file your cases efficiently.

## Getting Started

### 1. Registration

1. Visit the Nyaysetu homepage
2. Click "Get Started" or "Register"
3. Enter your email address
4. Create a secure password (minimum 8 characters)
5. Select "Citizen" as your role
6. Click "Register"

### 2. Login

1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your registered email and password
3. Click "Login"
4. You'll be redirected to your Citizen Portal dashboard

## Citizen Portal Overview

The Citizen Portal has four main sections accessible from the sidebar:

- **Dashboard**: Overview of your cases and quick actions
- **File Case**: Submit a new case
- **My Cases**: View and track all your filed cases
- **Help**: Support and contact information

## Filing a New Case

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Navigate to File Case**
   - Click "File Case" in the sidebar, OR
   - Click "File New Case" button on the dashboard

2. **Enter Case Details**
   - **Case Title** (required): Brief summary of your case (max 200 characters)
     - Example: "Property Boundary Dispute with Neighbor"
   - **Case Description** (required): Detailed explanation (max 10,000 characters)
     - Include all relevant facts, dates, and circumstances
     - Mention any evidence you have
   - **Category** (optional): Select the type of case
     - Options: Civil, Criminal, Family, Property, Commercial, Other

3. **Submit Your Case**
   - Review your information
   - Click "File Case"
   - You'll receive a confirmation with your Case ID

### What Happens Next?

After filing your case:

1. **AI Triage**: Your case is automatically analyzed by our AI system
2. **Priority Assignment**: Cases are assigned HIGH or REGULAR priority based on urgency
3. **Case Review**: Your case enters the queue for judicial review
4. **Updates**: Track your case status in the "My Cases" section

## Viewing Your Cases

### My Cases Section

1. Click "My Cases" in the sidebar
2. You'll see all your filed cases with:
   - Case title
   - Case ID
   - Status badge (FILED, IN_REVIEW, HEARING_SCHEDULED)
   - Priority badge (HIGH, REGULAR)
   - Filing date

### Filtering and Searching

- **Search**: Use the search bar to find cases by title
- **Filter by Status**: Select "All", "Filed", "In Review", or "Hearing Scheduled"

### Viewing Case Details

1. Click on any case card to expand it
2. View:
   - Full case description
   - AI-generated summary (if available)
   - Current status and priority
   - Filing date and case ID
3. Click again to collapse

## Understanding Case Status

- **FILED**: Your case has been submitted and is awaiting review
- **IN_REVIEW**: A judge is currently reviewing your case
- **HEARING_SCHEDULED**: Your case has progressed to the hearing stage

## Understanding Priority Levels

- **HIGH**: Urgent cases requiring immediate attention (e.g., assault, emergency, critical situations)
- **REGULAR**: Standard cases following normal processing timeline

## Dashboard Features

### Quick Stats

View at-a-glance metrics:
- Total cases filed
- Pending cases (FILED or IN_REVIEW)
- Resolved cases (HEARING_SCHEDULED)

### Recent Activity

See your 5 most recent cases with their current status

### Quick Actions

- **File New Case**: Jump directly to the case filing form
- **View My Cases**: See all your cases

## Tips for Filing Cases

1. **Be Specific**: Provide clear, detailed descriptions
2. **Include Dates**: Mention when events occurred
3. **List Evidence**: Note what documentation you have
4. **Use Keywords**: For urgent matters, use words like "urgent", "emergency", "immediate" to help with priority assignment
5. **Review Before Submit**: Double-check all information before filing

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Login?
- Verify your email and password are correct
- Ensure you registered as a "Citizen"
- Try resetting your password (contact support)

### Case Not Showing?
- Refresh the page
- Check your internet connection
- Verify you're logged in

### Need Help?
- Click "Help" in the sidebar
- Contact support at support@nyaysetu.com

## Privacy and Security

- Your cases are only visible to you and assigned judges
- All data is encrypted in transit
- Passwords are securely hashed
- Sessions expire after 24 hours for security
